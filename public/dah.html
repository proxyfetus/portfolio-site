Dah Dah Dah Dah Dah Dit Dah Dah Dah Dah (binary code 0 and 1 written in Morse code)

watercolor on paper
80 x 200 cm
2018

The title, “0” and “1”, the basic components in computational code written in morse code, is drawn in a horizontal line in the center of the painting. In contrast, the egg shaped abstract blobs can be depicted in various scales: from cosmic eggs, Platonic world image in alchemy, planets to microorganisms and embryos. The anonymosity of both elements refers to the unification of the micro and the macro and is linked by the algorithm terrain drawn in clear lines.
